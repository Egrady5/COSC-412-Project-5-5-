About Us

At FixMyRide, we believe car repair should be simple, transparent, and stress-free. That’s why we created a platform that connects you with trusted local mechanics who come to your location, whether you're at home, work, or stuck on the road. Our goal is to save you time and hassle by removing the need to visit a shop. From routine maintenance to urgent repairs, FixMyRide makes sure you get fast, reliable service from certified professionals. All with just a few clicks. We’re more than a convenience. We’re a community of car owners and mechanics working together to keep vehicles running smoothly. FixMyRide is your go-to destination for modern car care, where quality meets convenience.
